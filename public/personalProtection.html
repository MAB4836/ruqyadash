Emergency Protection (When Under Attack)

↑

When Feeling Sudden Sleepiness, Brain Fog, or Heaviness

Follow these steps immediately:

1. Break Immediate Influence

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنْكَ يَا عَدُوَّ اللَّهِ

A'udhu billahi minka ya 'aduww Allah

(I seek refuge in Allah from you, O enemy of Allah)

2. Direct Body Protection (3 times)

Put your hand over heart/head and say:

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ أَعُوذُ بِعِزَّةِ اللَّهِ وَقُدْرَتِهِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا أَجِدُ وَأُحَاذِرُ

Bismillah. A'udhu bi 'izzatillahi wa qudratihi min sharri ma ajidu wa uhadhir

(In the name of Allah. I seek refuge in the might and power of Allah from the evil of what I feel and what I fear.) - Prophetic du'a ﷺ

3-4. Powerful Expulsion

• Recite Ayat al-Kursi (2:255) silently or aloud
• Follow with Surah Al-Falaq and An-Nas

5-8. Clear & Exit

• Take 3 deep breaths saying "Bismillah, Allahu Akbar"
• Whisper "La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah" continuously
• Rub body lightly (head to chest) saying "Bismillah"
• Exit for fresh air and recite dhikr audibly

Emergency Tawakkul

حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

Ḥasbunallāhu wa niʿma al-wakīl

Allah is sufficient for us, and He is the best disposer of affairs.

- Quran 3:173, powerful against enemies and evil plots

When Feeling the Presence of Evil/Jinn

↑

When sensing evil presence

Say immediately:

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنْكَ، أَلْعَنُكَ بِلَعْنَةِ اللَّهِ

Aʿūdhu billāhi minka, alʿanuka bilaʿnati-llāh

I seek refuge in Allah from you. I curse you with the curse of Allah.

From the sunnah of dealing with Shayṭān when sensing his presence (Ahmad, authenticated by Ibn Taymiyyah).

When Afflicted or Frightened

↑

General Protection

Say with conviction:

حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

Ḥasbunallāhu wa niʿma al-wakīl

Allah is sufficient for us, and He is the best disposer of affairs.

Source: Qur'ān (3:173), powerful against enemies and evil plots.

1. Du'a for Pain – Place Hand on Area & Recite

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ
(3 times)

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ وَقُدْرَتِهِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا أَجِدُ وَأُحَاذِرُ
(7 times)

Bismillāh (3x)

Aʿūdhu billāhi wa qudratihi min sharri mā ajidu wa uḥādhir (7x)

In the Name of Allah (3x). I seek refuge in Allah and His power from the evil of what I feel and what I fear.

Action: Place your right hand on the area of pain. Say "Bismillah" three times, then say the full du'a seven times.

Source: Ṣaḥīḥ Muslim (Hadith 2202) — Narrated by ʿUthmān ibn Abī al-ʿĀṣ

2. Du'a of Jibrīl (ʿalayhis-salām) for Healing

اللَّهُمَّ رَبَّ النَّاسِ، أَذْهِبِ الْبَأْسَ، اشْفِ أَنْتَ الشَّافِي، لَا شِفَاءَ إِلَّا شِفَاؤُكَ، شِفَاءً لَا يُغَادِرُ سَقَمًا

Allāhumma rabba-n-nās, adhhibi-l-baʾsa, ishfi anta ash-shāfī, lā shifāʾa illā shifāʾuka, shifāʾan lā yughadiru saqaman

O Allah, Lord of mankind, remove the harm and heal. You are the Healer; there is no healing except Your healing — a healing that leaves no disease behind.

Source: Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī and Muslim – Used by the Prophet ﷺ when treating the sick.

3. General Du'a for Shifāʾ (Can Be Said Anytime)

اللَّهُمَّ اشْفِنِي شِفَاءً تَامًّا، شِفَاءً لَا يُغَادِرُ سَقَمًا

Allāhumma ishfinī shifāʾan tāmmā, shifāʾan lā yughadiru saqaman

O Allah, grant me complete healing — a healing that leaves behind no illness.

Before Sleep Protection

↑

Essential Night Protection

• Recite Ayat al-Kursi (2:255) - the greatest verse of protection

• Recite Surah Al-Falaq and Surah An-Nas, then blow over your entire body starting from your head

Ayat al-Kursi before sleep protects you all night. No jinn or shaytan can approach you until morning.

Spiritual Cleansing Du'a

Say this every night:

"O Allah, if there is anything in me not from You, remove it. If there is anything bound to me through sihr, touch, or evil intention, release it and burn it. I belong to You and You alone."

Truth & Justice Du'a

O Allah, show me truth as truth and grant me the ability to follow it. Show me falsehood as falsehood and grant me the ability to avoid it. O Allah, whoever has wished harm upon me, turn their plot against themselves, expose them, and return their magic upon them—without harm to me.

Sleep Guidelines

• Maintain wudu before sleep • Keep your heart focused on Allah—not revenge or blame • Remember: "And they plan, and Allah plans. And Allah is the best of planners." (Quran 8:30)

If Waking from a Nightmare or Disturbance

↑

Protection from Night Disturbances

Say immediately:

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ غَضَبِهِ وَعِقَابِهِ وَشَرِّ عِبَادِهِ وَمِنْ هَمَزَاتِ الشَّيَاطِينِ وَأَنْ يَحْضُرُونِ

Aʿūdhu bikalimāti-llāhi at-tāmmāti min ghaḍabihī wa ʿiqābihī wa sharri ʿibādihī wa min hamazāti ash-shayāṭīn wa an yaḥḍurūn

I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from His anger, His punishment, the evil of His slaves, the whisperings of devils, and their coming near me.

Source: Ibn Mājah

Against Sihr/Magic

↑

Breaking Family/Marriage Sihr

Say with conviction:

O Allah, nullify every sihr intended to ruin my bond with my family and loved ones.

Destroy & Burn Sihr

O Allah, destroy the sihr, burn the jinn, and remove them with Your power.

Block Sihr Renewal

O Allah, nullify every sihr that is being renewed upon me. Cut every link between me and those who renew it. Lift all harm and affliction from me. And grant me a pure heart that loves You, fears You, and hopes only in You.

Recite after every prayer, especially in sujood and Qiyam al-Layl

Breaking False Comfort

When intrusive thoughts come, say aloud or mentally: "This is not from me. This is an attack. Ya Allah, I return this to You. I cut this tie for Your sake." Repeat as needed - each time weakens the spiritual chain.

Protection from Hidden Enemies

↑

When You Suspect Someone Harmed You

Say with trust in Allah:

"Ya Allah, if anyone has harmed me, You are Al-Adl (The Just), remove it and return it to them without harming me."

General Protection from Enemies

اللَّهُمَّ اكْفِنِيهِمْ بِمَا شِئْتَ

Allāhumma ikfinīhim bimā shiʾta

O Allah, protect me from them however You will.

- Sahih Muslim, du'a of Prophet ﷺ when threatened

Divine Justice Du'a

O Allah, show me truth as truth and grant me to follow it. Show me falsehood as falsehood and grant me to avoid it. O Allah, whoever wishes harm upon me, turn their plot against themselves, expose them, and return their magic upon them—without harm to me.

Trust Allah's Perfect Plan

Quran 8:30: "And they plan, and Allah plans. And Allah is the best of planners."

Quran 14:42: "And never think that Allah is unaware of what the wrongdoers do. He only delays them for a Day when eyes will stare in horror."

Keep your healing focused on Allah—not revenge. He will expose them in due time.

Entering Suspect Places

↑

Entry Du'a (Any House)

Say before entering:

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَ الْمَوْلَجِ وَخَيْرَ الْمَخْرَجِ، بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَلَجْنَا، وَبِسْمِ اللَّهِ خَرَجْنَا، وَعَلَى اللَّهِ رَبِّنَا تَوَكَّلْنَا

Allahumma inni as'aluka khayr al-mawlaji wa khayr al-makhraji, bismillahi walajna, wa bismillahi kharajna, wa 'ala Allah rabbina tawakkalna

(O Allah, I ask You for the goodness of entering and the goodness of exiting. In the name of Allah we enter, and in the name of Allah we leave, and upon our Lord we place our trust.) - Sunan Abi Dawood

Pocket Du'a Card

Keep memorized or carry:

بِسْمِ اللهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللهِ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللهِ

Bismillahi tawakkaltu 'ala Allah, wa la hawla wa la quwwata illa billah

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ

A'udhu bikalimatillahi at-taammati min sharri ma khalaq (3x)

Emergency Response

If you feel sudden symptoms (sleepiness, fog, heaviness):

1. A'udhu billahi minka ya 'aduww Allah
2. A'udhu bi 'izzatillahi... (3x with hand on heart)
3. Ayat al-Kursi + Falaq + Nas
4. Exit for fresh air and dhikr

Exit & Cleansing

• Leave calmly while reciting dhikr audibly
• Take deep breaths of fresh air
• Continue protective dhikr until symptoms clear
• Break environmental spiritual control

Daily Morning & Evening Protection

↑

Morning & Evening (Essential)

Recite 3 times morning and evening:

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ

Bismillahilladhi la yadurru ma'a ismihi shay'un fil-ardi wa laa fissamaa'i wa huwa as-Sami'ul-'Aleem

In the name of Allah, with whose name nothing can cause harm in the earth or the heavens, and He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing.

(Recite 3 times morning and evening)

Tawakkul Du'a (7 times each)

حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ

Hasbiyallahu la ilaha illa Huwa, 'alayhi tawakkaltu wa Huwa Rabb ul-'Arsh il-'Azeem

(Allah is sufficient for me, there is no god but He; I place my trust in Him, and He is the Lord of the Mighty Throne.)

General Protection

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ

A'udhu bikalimatillahi at-taammati min sharri ma khalaq

(I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He created.)

Against Anxiety & Weakness

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحَزَنِ وَالْعَجْزِ وَالْكَسَلِ وَالْجُبْنِ وَالْبُخْلِ وَغَلَبَةِ الدَّيْنِ وَقَهْرِ الرِّجَالِ

Allahumma inni a'udhu bika min al-hammi wal-hazan, wal-'ajzi wal-kasal, wal-jubni wal-bukhl, wa ghalabat id-dayni wa qahr ir-rijal

(O Allah, I seek refuge in You from anxiety and sorrow, weakness and laziness, cowardice and miserliness, and from being overpowered by men and jinn.) - Bukhari
